As a Product Manager, having a core philosophy for your app makes it much easier to pitch to investors
 or employers. You can now say:

"The app is called Pal-Lock. 'Pal' represents the personal network and 
trust. 'Lock' is based on the Positive Action Lock system—meaning security 
and payments are frictionless and happen automatically by default." 



A Pal-Lock (Positive Action Lock):

Locks automatically when action is completed
Requires intentional action to unlock
Creates safety without friction
